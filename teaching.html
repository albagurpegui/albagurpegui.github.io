Teaching



“Experience is simply the name we give our mistakes.” —Oscar Wilde

During my studies, I always found teaching inspiring and rewarding. I believe there is so much to learn from how others learn, and I see collaborative learning as one of the most valuable aspects of academia. I consider myself a learning enthusiast. Studying has helped me in most aspects of my life, and I always try to share that energy with my students, along with understanding and empathy. I think it is important not only to help students acquire the knowledge they need to succeed, but also to adapt to their different needs, offer varied pedagogical strategies, and most importantly, encourage them to ask questions openly and confidently.
